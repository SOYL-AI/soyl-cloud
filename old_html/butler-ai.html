Frequently Asked Questions

Do guests need to download an app?

No. Butler AI is entirely web-based. Guests simply scan a QR code in their room to instantly access the platform on their own device browser.

Does it integrate with our PMS?

Yes, Butler AI integrates with major PMS providers to verify guest status, automatically pull in guest names, and post charges directly to the room folio.

How does staff receive requests?

Staff can receive requests via our dedicated staff dashboard on tablets, desktop, or mobile devices. Notifications can also be routed via email or SMS.
